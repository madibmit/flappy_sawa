Privacy Policy for Flappy Sawa

Last updated: April 29, 2024

1. Information We Collect

Flappy Sawa collects the following information:

  • Game progress and high scores
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Usage statistics (game sessions, features used)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Maintain your game progress
  • Display leaderboard rankings
  • Improve game performance and features
  • Provide customer support

3. Data Storage

Your game data is stored locally on your device. High scores are stored on our secure servers for leaderboard purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Services (for achievements and leaderboards)
  • Firebase Analytics (for game improvement)

5. Children's Privacy

Our game is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
